QUESTIONS

Common Questions.

How long does a wrap last?

Premium cast vinyl wraps last 5-7 years with normal commercial use. Standard calendared vinyl lasts 3-5 years. The wrap survives normal weather, washes, and highway miles — the brand investment outlasts most ad spend.

Who owns the design?

You do. We can install your existing brand assets, work with your designer, or refer you to a Georgia wrap design partner. The creative is yours; we handle the install and the miles.

What if I want to change the design mid-contract?

Mid-contract design changes are possible but cost the same as a fresh install. Most advertisers commit to one design for the full term to maximize brand recognition.

Do you report impressions?

Yes. Monthly reports include daily miles driven, route map, and impression estimates calibrated against Geopath-style methodology (vehicles per route × density factors). Estimated, not exact — but consistent.

Can I run a co-branded wrap with BB Fleets?

Yes. Many advertisers prefer a co-branded design where their logo and message share space with the BB Fleets authority. It often increases trust signals and reduces wrap cost slightly because we share design space.

What industries don't fit?

Adult content, gambling, anything in active litigation, and direct competitors to our trucking operations. Otherwise — banks, law firms, restaurants, healthcare, B2B SaaS, real estate, local services — all welcome.
